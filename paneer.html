Paneer masala

INGREDIENTS

  • 200 gm cottage cheese
  • 2 inches chopped ginger
  • 12 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/3 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered black pepper
  • 3 tablespoon refined oil/li>
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 chopped green chilli
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/3 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/3 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 2 pinches salt

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, fry it for few seconds on medium flame.
    2. Add ginger, chopped green chilli and garlic, fry for a few minutes on medium flame.
    3. Put chopped onion and fry it for 2 to 3 minutes on medium flame, keep stirring in between.
    4. Now add chopped tomato and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes on medium flame, keep stirring in between.
    5. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder and black pepper powder.
    6. Mix it and cook the spices for a minute on low flame.
    7. Add paneer and salt. Mix it and cook for 2 minutes on low flame (cover the lid).
      Add garam masala powder and slitted green chilli, mix it and turn off the flame.
